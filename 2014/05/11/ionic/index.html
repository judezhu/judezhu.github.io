Why use ionic?

Do like to use framework? I’m not sure how people actually see frameworks. Sometimes, framework are really pain on the ass. There is steep learning curve for some frameworks such as spring. However, a good framework can save you tons of work from starting from the scratch.

When it comes to the front-end part, a good framework can make sure you have a decent app no matter how bad you are at front-end stuff. Probably with one day work, you can have something looks professional to show to other people. That’s why I like ionic.
