Why use ionic?

11May

Do like to use frameworks? I’m not sure how people actually see frameworks. Sometimes, frameworks are really pain on the ass. There are steep learning curves for some frameworks such as spring. However, a good framework can really save you tons of work from starting from the scratch.

When it comes to the front-end part, a good front-end framework can make sure you have a decent app no matter how bad you are at front-end stuff. Probably with one day work, you can have something which looks professional to show. That’s why I like ionic.
